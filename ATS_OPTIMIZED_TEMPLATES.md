## Key ATS Optimization Features in These Templates

✅ **Single-column layout** - No tables, text boxes, or columns
✅ **Standard headings** - SUMMARY, SKILLS, EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, etc.
✅ **Contact info in body** - Not in header/footer
✅ **Simple bullets** - Using standard bullet points (•)
✅ **Consistent date format** - MM/YYYY throughout
✅ **Action verbs** - Led, Built, Designed, Implemented, Analyzed
✅ **Quantified results** - Metrics in every bullet (%, $, time saved)
✅ **Keyword-rich** - Technical skills, tools, and domain terms
✅ **Safe fonts** - Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman (10-12pt)
✅ **ATS-friendly file format** - Save as .DOCX (preferred) or standard PDF

---

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Choose the right template** for your experience level
2. **Copy into Word/Google Docs** and set font to Arial or Calibri, 10-11pt
3. **Replace placeholder content** with your actual experience
4. **Mirror job description keywords** - Extract 10-15 key terms from the JD and naturally incorporate them
5. **Quantify everything** - Add metrics to every bullet (%, time, money, users)
6. **Keep it concise** - 1 page for <5 years experience, 2 pages for 5+ years
7. **Save as DOCX** - Most ATS-compatible format
8. **Test with ATS checker** - Use Jobscan or similar tool to verify

---

## Template Customization Tips

**For Tech Roles:** Emphasize programming languages, frameworks, and tools in SKILLS section. Include GitHub/portfolio links.

**For Data Roles:** Focus on SQL, Python, visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI), and statistical analysis. Include data project examples.

**For PM Roles:** Highlight stakeholder management, roadmapping, metrics/KPIs, and cross-functional leadership. Show business impact.

**For Career Switchers:** Lead with transferable skills and relevant projects. De-emphasize unrelated experience but show progression.
